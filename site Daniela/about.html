Set 700 m from Agia Fotini Church, Mythic Hotel in Paralia Katerinis has a seasonal outdoor swimming pool and garden.

Boasting a 24-hour front desk. Both free WiFi and private parking are accessible at this property.

At the hotel, the rooms include a wardrobe, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom. Rooms have air conditioning, and certain rooms at Mythic Hotel have a balcony.

The daily breakfast offers continental and buffet options. Guests can dine in the on-site restaurant, which specialises in Greek cuisine.

The nearest airport is Thessaloniki Airport, 100 km from Mythic Hotel.
